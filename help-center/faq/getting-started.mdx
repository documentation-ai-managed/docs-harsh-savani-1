## Starting Guide

Sign up at `https://docs.harshsavani.com/register`.

1. Verify your email after registration.
2. Log in to your dashboard.
3. Click "Create New Project".
4. Follow prompts to set project name and details.
5. Start writing docs using the online editor.

Need more help? Contact `support@harshsavani.com`.

## Add a New Doc

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    From your dashboard, select a project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Add Page'">
    Use the sidebar button labeled 'Add Page'.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in details">
    Enter title and content. You can use Markdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and publish">
    Click 'Save' then 'Publish' to make it public.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Overview

Harsh Savani provides a centralized space to organize, manage, and collaborate on project documentation. You can create structured docs for software projects, internal wikis, or client deliverables with ease. Build knowledge bases that scale with your teams, featuring search, versioning, and sharing capabilities.

The platform supports Markdown editing, embeds, and custom layouts, making it ideal for developers, product managers, and technical writers.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://harshsavani.com` and sign up with your email.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Use Google or GitHub for faster onboarding.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step title="New Workspace" icon="plus">
    Click "New Workspace" and name your project, like "MyApp Docs".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add First Page" icon="file-text">
    Create a page with Markdown:

````markdown
# Welcome to MyApp

This is your first documentation page.

- [Quickstart](/quickstart)
- [API Reference](/api)
````

  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish & Share" icon="share-2">
    Hit publish and share the live URL with your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
